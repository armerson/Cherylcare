# CherylCare — Future Feature Roadmap

## v1.1 — Depth (Months 2–3)

### Nutrition and Lifestyle Tracking
- Water intake with quick-add buttons (250ml increments)
- Protein tracker with food log
- Caffeine intake log
- Alcohol units tracker
- Exercise log with type and intensity
- Lifestyle insights engine

### Enhanced Physio
- Exercise library (50+ pre-built exercises for hEDS)
- Video/image demonstrations per exercise
- Physiotherapist-guided plan templates
- Export physio session summary as PDF

### PMDD Enhanced
- PMDD severity scoring (validated DSM-5 scale)
- GP-ready PMDD symptom report export
- Cycle comparison charts (overlay multiple cycles)

---

## v1.2 — Health Export (Month 4)

### Report Generation
- Monthly health summary PDF
- PMDD pattern report (shareable with GP/gynaecologist)
- Physio progress report (shareable with physiotherapist)
- Supplement adherence table
- Cycle history chart
- Trigger: user-initiated from Settings > Export

### Data Management
- Full data export (JSON)
- Account deletion with full data wipe
- Data backup reminder

---

## v1.3 — Wearable Integration (Month 5–6)

### Apple Health Integration
- Import: sleep data, heart rate, step count
- Export: period data to Apple Health
- Resting heart rate as proxy for recovery

### Google Fit Integration
- Import: activity, sleep
- Passive data reduces manual entry burden

---

## v2.0 — Intelligence (Months 6–12)

### Enhanced AI Insights
- LLM-powered natural language insight summaries (Claude API)
- Personalised self-care suggestions based on patterns
- Trend forecasting: "Based on your cycle, the next 3 days may be challenging"
- Anomaly detection: "This cycle looks different to your usual pattern"

### Predictive Features
- Predicted PMDD severity for upcoming window (based on sleep/stress)
- "Best days this cycle" calendar view
- Energy forecast by cycle phase

---

## v2.1 — Community and Support (Year 2)

### Healthcare Provider Mode
- Share-code for physiotherapist/GP access
- Read-only portal for healthcare team
- Appointment tracking and note-taking
- Medication change logging (with healthcare provider)

### Education Library
- In-depth articles on hEDS, PMDD, hypermobility
- Curated resource library (books, podcasts, organisations)
- Phase-relevant self-care suggestions

### Partner/Support Person Mode
- Opt-in sharing of mood/PMDD status with trusted person
- Notification to partner: "Cheryl is in her PMDD window"
- Privacy-controlled (user chooses what to share)

---

## v3.0 — Clinical Integration (Year 2–3)

### Telehealth
- In-app consultation booking
- Pre-appointment symptom summary
- Post-appointment action tracking

### Research Participation
- Opt-in anonymised data contribution to hEDS/PMDD research
- Research insights returned to user

### Validated Scales
- COPE scale for pain
- PRISM (PMDD validated assessment)
- Beighton Score tracking (hypermobility assessment)
